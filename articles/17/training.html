M1, M2, M3 Training problems

Do as many of these problems as you can. If you can’t do one, skip it and try it again later. Above all, don’t panic. Some questions seem hard to understand but dealing with that is part of the training. Every time you complete a problem, you become better. Problems that seem hard today will seem easy after training.
